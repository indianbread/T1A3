About me

I am a pharmacist currently in the process of a career change and retraining to become a full stack developer.

I have been a long-time fan of the Korean entertainment industry. I enjoy listening to Korean music as well as watching Korean dramas in my spare time.

My favourite past time with friends is playing board games. My most favourite board game is Carcasonne, a strategy-based tile game.

I welcome any networking and professional development opportunies. Once I have more experience in the industry I would like to participate in organising a few tech meet ups.

I am passionate about technology and how it has the power to change the way we work. I experienced first hand how technology positively improves workflows in the retail pharmacy space as well as in hospitals. In the near future I hope to contribute to technology that makes a large positive impact on an industry, be it health or something else.
